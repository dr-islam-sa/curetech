CureTech Uses variety of  Biomaterials designed for precision, safety, and clinical performance

Medical Grade Pure PEEK

For high-demand Regular Implant components, we use Pure Polyetheretherketone (PEEK), a high-performance thermoplastic known for its exceptional thermal and chemical stability.
This material is USP Class VI and ASTM F2026-2017 compliant, meeting the highest global standards for surgical implantable materials
It has successfully passed ISO 10993-5 cytotoxicity testing and is free from heavy metal contamination . With a melting point of approximately 340°C and high flexural strength, it provides a reliable, high-purity alternative for specialized medical and surgical devices.

Medical Grade Titanium (Ti-6Al-4V ELI)

We Uses high-purity Ti-6Al-4V ELI (Extra Low Interstitials) titanium alloy, a premier choice for medical and dental implants due to its superior biocompatibility, corrosion resistance, and high specific strength .
The material is rigorously tested and complies with international standards, including ASTM F136, ISO 5832-3, and AMS 4911R, ensuring it meets the strict mechanical and chemical requirements for surgical applications.
Its excellent fatigue resistance and reliability make it ideal for permanent clinical use, providing patients with safe and durable long-term solutions. 

High-Impact Durable Biocompatible Resin

Biocompatible Resin is a clear, high-performance material specifically designed for medical devices requiring exceptional impact, shatter, and abrasion resistance . Produced in an ISO 13485 facility, it is USP Class VI certified and has passed rigorous ISO 10993-5, 10, and 11 testing for cytotoxicity, irritation, and systemic toxicity.
Its unique properties allow for long-term skin contact (exceeding 30 days) and short-term contact with bone, tissue, and dentin, making it a versatile choice for patient-specific surgical instruments and robust medical components . 

Rigid Amber Biocompatible Resin

This Rigid Biocompatible Resin is an specialized material engineered for short-term contact applications, such as surgical planning and implant sizing tools . It offers high tensile and flexural strength, ensuring precision and stability during clinical use . The material is manufactured under ISO 13485 standards and is fully compliant with ISO 10993-5, 10, and 11, as well as ISO 7405 for dental applications.
Additionally, it is highly adaptable to clinical environments, maintaining its integrity through common sterilization methods, including Autoclave, Ethylene Oxide, and Gamma radiation.
