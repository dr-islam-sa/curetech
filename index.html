CureTech is a leading provider of patient-specific cranio-maxillofacial (CMF) solutions in Oman. We specialize in high-precision, custom-made implants for the skull, face, and jaw, combining advanced medical 3D printing, virtual surgical planning (VSP), and personalized design. By collaborating closely with surgeons, we transform complex anatomical challenges into successful outcomes, delivering reliable, safe, and innovative patient-specific implants for cranial, facial, and orthopedic reconstruction.

13485:2016 Certified By SGS

At CureTech , we are committed to delivering high-quality medical devices that meet customer requirements and comply with ISO 13485:2016 and EU MDR (2017/745) standards.
