Features

- PEEK implants match bone contours perfectly.
- Lower re-operation rates for patients.
- Fits complex areas like posterior ledge & infraorbital rim.
- Fewer complications and infections than titanium or bone.
- Better aesthetic results than titanium implants.
- Most cost-effective compared to PMMA and titanium.
- Designed and manufactured in 10 business days in Oman.
