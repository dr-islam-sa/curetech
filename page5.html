Cranial Implants

Connect, innovate, succeed

Our cranial implants are designed to address complex cranial defects with custom-made precision. Our implants provide a permanent solution that integrates seamlessly with the patient’s unique anatomy. These implants are fabricated using state-of-the-art CNC technology and PEEK medical grade polymer, tailored specifically to the patient’s cranial structure.

They offer superior aesthetic outcomes by matching the natural contours of the skull, improving both function and appearance. Custom implants also enhance the protection of underlying brain tissue, provide mechanical strength, and reduce the risk of complications associated with traditional methods.

Furthermore, our implants are designed with fixation points that ensure stable integration with the remaining bone. They can accommodate various cranial defect types, from small localized areas to more extensive defects crossing anatomical boundaries

Using such a cutting guide in a two-in-one operation has great advantages:


- Shorter theatre time
- Shorter anaesthesia
- Quicker recovery
- Fewer associated costs
