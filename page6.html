Subperiosteal Implants

We design and manufacture patient specific implants using advanced digital workflows, medical imaging, and precision manufacturing. Our products include cranial, maxillofacial.

Connect, innovate, succeed

Curetech offers custom-made 3D printed subperiosteal dental implants. This system offers an alternative to conventional endosseous implants for patients with severely atrophied mandible and maxilla bone. The implants are secured with sufficient screws at the optimal bone sites to eliminate any micro movement. The implants are accompanied by matching titanium custom cutting and drilling guides to ensure the correct position and perfect fit.
