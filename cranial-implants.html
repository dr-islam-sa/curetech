Cranial Implants

Features

Patient-Specific Design and Biocompatibility

Our cranial implants are manufactured from medical grade PEEK, a biocompatible polymer widely used in advanced cranial reconstruction. Using precise CNC milling, each implant is produced according to the patient specific anatomy, ensuring accurate fit, structural stability, and reliable performance during surgery and long term clinical use.

Lightweight Strength and Surgical Reliability

PEEK implants are lightweight yet mechanically strong, offering excellent durability while maintaining patient comfort. The material is radiolucent, allowing clear postoperative imaging without significant artifacts. Combined with high precision CNC manufacturing, this enables consistent quality, smooth surface finishing, and dependable fixation with standard cranial screws in surgery.
